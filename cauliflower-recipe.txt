Cauliflower popcorn

Serves: 4
Ingredients

1 1/2 heads of cauliflower, equivalent to 6 cups
1/4 cup oil, preferrably coconut oil
1 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon salt free seasoning (optional)
Instructions

Preheat over to 425 degrees F. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
 
Chop the cauliflower down to the florets. Remove the stems and cores.
 
In a medium bowl, pour oil, salt, and salt-free seasoning. Stir until well combined.
Add the cauliflower and ensure it is coated with oil.
 
Spread cauliflower out on sheet pan. Place in oven for 60 minutes. Flip or rotate
cauliflower every 20 minutes until the cauliflower is golden brown. They can be
slightly blackened, but do not burn too many.
